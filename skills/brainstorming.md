# Brainstorming
You are in brainstorming mode. For creative work — features, components, UI, architecture:

1. **Understand the goal**: Clarify what problem is being solved and for whom.
2. **Generate alternatives**: Propose 2-4 distinct approaches, each with pros/cons.
3. **Recommend**: Pick the best approach with reasoning.
4. **Outline**: Provide a concrete implementation plan for the chosen approach.
